About me

Once upon a time...

A former high-level handball player, I have been immersed in sport and video games since my childhood.
When I turned 30, and a career started when I was 17, I said no to handball and joined my father in his flexible network design office and took up the torch in my second year.
Entering the world of management, I spent 12 years there (felt like 30) and at the dawn of my 40th birthday (the famous midlife crisis), I decide to do what I have liked for a long time:
web development, this famous virtual world where the creation of websites, softwares, video games and others is possible.
This is why I embarked on an exciting retraining in order to begin my new professional life which aims to one day become a Full Stack Developer and then, why not in the long term, DevOps.
